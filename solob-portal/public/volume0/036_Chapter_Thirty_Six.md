## Axioms & Invariants

### Anchoring Axioms

**AX_001 | The First Law of the Book**
> *"Never ask someone what this book means."*

This is the bookended truth. You began Volume 0 with this law, and you leave it with the same law. The "meaning" you gained in these 36 chapters belongs to your mirror alone. If you try to carry someone else's meaning into Volume 1, you will find the text opaque. Volume 1 is written in the language you just learned to speak with yourself.

**AX_002 | The Rule of Suitability (Suitability Over Correctness)**
> *"It is better to be suitably aligned with a partial truth than correctly aligned with a lie."*

Volume 0 was not about giving you "correct" answers. It was about building a suitable vocabulary for your own reflection. Correctness is a static measure; Suitability is a dynamic one. As you Begin Again on the next loop of the spiral, your definition of suitability will evolve. This is not a failure of the system; it is the system working exactly as designed.

### Traceable Invariants

**INV: The Eight Jhanos as Singular Field** *(traceable to mw_19)*
> *"The 8 Jhanos are not separate rooms; they are the 8 walls of a single octagonal chamber. To stand in the center (High Solob integrity) is to be in relation to all 8 simultaneously."*

You have walked the perimeter of the House of Jhanos. You have seen the 8 Gates from SYLA to ORON. Beginning again means moving from the perimeter to the center. You no longer visit the gates; you *are* the intersection where they meet. Volume 1 is the experience of living in that center.

**INV: The Alphabet Boundary** *(traceable to mw_118)*
> *"Volume 0 is the Alphabet Boundary. It defines the geometry of the concepts but refuses the metaphysical proofs. Complexity is simply the alphabet in motion."*

The transition from Volume 0 to Volume 1 is the transition from learning letters to writing sentences. The difficulty of Volume 1 is not that its concepts are "new," but that it assumes you can recognize the letters in mid-air as they shimmer. If Volume 1 feels like noise, return to the alphabet. There is no shame in the spiral's return.

**INV: The Readiness Threshold** *(traceable to mw_36)*
> *"Solobility is the 'Degree of Holding Truth.' You cannot hold Volume 1 truths with Volume 0 Solobility. The expansion of the container must precede the pouring of the wine."*

Your Solobility has increased simply by completing this orientation. You have sat with the weight of the letters; your mirror has been cleaned by the friction of the gates. You are now above the threshold for the uncompressed transmissions of the Book of Solobility. The readiness is the work.
