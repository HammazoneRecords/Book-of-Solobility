## Glyphs

Symbols, sounds, shapes, or archetypes that act as "Zip Files of Emotion." They compress infinite, abstract meaning into a singular, observable form. A glyph is not a word; it is the container for a frequency.

When a word is spoken, it transfers data. When a glyph is activated, it transfers a state of being. The 8 Jhanos Gates are the primary glyphs of the Solobverse, acting as the structural code of reality.

## Drayl (D-R-A-Y-L)

The internal, non-linear archive that lives behind the eyelids. It is the raw material of memory and emotion before it is processed by logic. A Drayl is not a singular event; it is a "layer" in the geological stratum of your consciousness.

Drayls cannot be integrated or resolved in the traditional human sense; they can only be layered over. The heavier the Drayl, the more mass it exerts on the Solob, bending the light of your perception toward its own gravity.

## Tri-Element Formula of a Drayl

A true Drayl is formed by the collision of three distinct elements, requiring the **Power of the Third** to lock into reality:
1.  **Event (The Strike):** The raw kinetic action in space-time. (Observation)
2.  **Emotion (The Heat):** The internal somatic response to the strike. (Reflection)
3.  **Meaning (The Glyph):** The container chosen to hold the heat. (Pattern/Opinion)

When these three align, a Drayl is forged. It leaves the realm of temporary experience and immediately sinks into the Solobic Neural Web.

## The Solobic Neural Web

The "Internet of the Cosmos." The infinite, interconnected mycelial-like network that stores and transmits all Drayls, experiences, and Solobic truths across Time and Space. It is the uncompressed database of existence. 

When you experience a shimmer, you are not inventing a new thought. You are downloading a ping from the Neural Web that resonated with the exact shape of your current Drayl.

### The Mathematics of Memory

At '4' (Sequential Confirmation), the universe realizes that some things never change. A Drayl is the physical proof of 4. When you hold a heavy memory, you are not holding the past. You are holding a confirmed structure. 

The goal of Solobility is not to erase heavy Drayls, but to build a Mirror strong enough so that the weight of the Drayl does not crack the glass.
