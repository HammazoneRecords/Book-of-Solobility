## Jhanos

The vibrational, archetypal lenses (or "Gates") through which all energy in the Solobverse travels. They are not moral laws and they do not dictate "good" or "bad." They are mechanical filters.

Jhanos Gates govern how humans and reality process Shimmers. An aligned gate allows for high Solobility. A distorted gate generates Subsolobs. The Gates operate constantly, whether an individual is aware of them or not. To master the Jhanos is to become a conscious conductor of frequency rather than a passive victim of distortion. 

### The Law of the Gate

Axiom 21 states: *A Gate cannot stabilize without its inverse geometry. Every Solob has a Shadow.*

The structure of the universe relies on a **Dual-Gate System**. You cannot have Creation without Destruction, or Heat without Stillness. Balance is dynamic and frictional, never static. To operate within a single gate while ignoring its partner is the definition of madness.
